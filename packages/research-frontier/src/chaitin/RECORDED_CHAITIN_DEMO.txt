CLAIM BOUNDARY: Kolmogorov complexity is uncomputable; nothing here measures
it or detects an 'outcomplexed' evaluator. WITHIN_BUDGET certifies a
toolchain-relative compression bound under an explicit policy budget.
EVALUATION_UNDECIDABLE records only that a payload was not certified
comprehensible — never that it is complex or malicious. Receipts are signed
with a dev-only HMAC; their assurance is the replayable compression witness.
This module is standalone research code; no enforcement-runtime coupling,
no hardware attestation, and no throughput property is claimed.
